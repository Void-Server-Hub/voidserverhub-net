How It Works

The process for nominating and selecting winners each month

1

Nominations

Members would create a support ticket and pay for a nomination. Minimum entry fee is 10 dollars. Top 3 paid gets premium nomination, rest are in the announcement.

2

Submitions

All normal submitions are taken almost the entire month until last week arives.

3

Review

Our staff team reviews the top nominees and otm based on votes and community feedback.

4

Announcement

Winners are announced at the beginning of the following month with special recognition.
