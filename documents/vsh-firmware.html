How It Works

Transparent By Design

Understanding how VSH Firmware operates in your server

Centralized Management

VSH Firmware is intentionally and fully managed by XxVoidicxX in every server it runs in. It always has Administrator permissions, and I (Voidic) retain full control to execute moderation commands, run custom code, and push updates at any time.

This is by design, not deception. The bot was originally built for the Void Server Hub (VSH) and is deeply integrated into its network.

However, others are welcome to use it — with the understanding that I maintain central control for security, consistency, and updates. If you're uncomfortable with this level of access, you should not use this bot. If you want VSH-level automated protection, tools, and filtering, this is what powers it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about VSH Firmware

Is the bot backdoored?

No — or at least, not in the traditional or malicious sense.

Can I restrict your access to my server?

No. If you want a bot that can be limited or fully self-managed, this isn't the right one. VSH Firmware runs as a network-controlled service, not a hosted bot with server-level configs.

Can I suggest features or request support?

Yes! While I retain control, I'm open to suggestions or feature requests. You can open a ticket or DM me directly on Discord.

How often is the bot updated?

VSH Firmware receives regular updates for both features and security. Updates are pushed automatically to all servers using the bot.

What data does the bot collect?

The bot collects necessary operational data (message logs for moderation, user IDs for warnings/bans, etc.) but does not sell or share this data with third parties. All data is used solely for bot functionality.
