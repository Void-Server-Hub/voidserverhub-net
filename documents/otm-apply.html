You're Invited to Compete!

You're seeing this page because you've been offered the opportunity to participate in Void Server Hub's prestigious Of The Month (OTM) competitions. This is your chance to showcase your Minecraft achievements to our vibrant community!

What is VSH OTM? Each month, we recognize outstanding members of our community across various categories. These awards highlight the creativity, dedication, and passion that make our Minecraft network special. At the end of each month, community members vote to determine the winners in each category.

Pro Tip: While not required, we highly recommend encouraging your community members to join VSH to support your nomination. More voters means better chances of winning! Just remember to follow our VSH Standard when promoting your entry.

Apply via Events Tickets

Requirements:

  • All servers/users must comply with Discord's Terms of Service
  • Must adhere to the VSH Standard (see below)
  • Each category has specific eligibility requirements

What is the VSH Standard?

The VSH Standard is our community guidelines that ensure quality, respect, and a fun experience for everyone. All participants must follow these standards.

Read VSH Standard

Category-Specific Rules:

  • Realm OTM: For Minecraft Realms only!
  • Server OTM: For dedicated servers only!
  • Developer OTM: Open to anyone developing Minecraft-related content
  • Builder OTM: Showcase your best builds (private or public)
  • Addon OTM: Must be the original creator or significant contributor
