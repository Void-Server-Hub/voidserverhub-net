VOID SERVER HUB: VIOLATION TIER TERMS OF SERVICE (TOS)
Last updated: May 24, 2025

Guilt Principles

  • If someone is accused by others, they are treated as innocent until proven guilty.
  • If someone admits or jokes about serious offenses, they are treated as guilty until proven innocent, even if unverifiable.

Violation Timeout Policy

Old violations may be ignored if enough time has passed without repeated offenses. This does not remove them from logs, but staff may treat them as resolved.

  • Level 1: Ignored after 30 days
  • Level 2: Ignored after 60 days
  • Level 3: Ignored after 90 days (unless staff-related)
  • Level 4-5: Never ignored

STAFF VIOLATION MULTIPLIER

If a staff member breaks a rule, it is counted one level higher than normal.

This applies to all staff roles from Trial Mod to Head Admin.

Staff violations cannot escalate above Level 5.

Example:

  • A member using slurs = Level 1 ‚Üí Staff = Level 2
  • A member bypassing a ban = Level 2 ‚Üí Staff = Level 3
