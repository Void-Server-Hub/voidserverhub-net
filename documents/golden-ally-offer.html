Golden Ally Requirements

To become a Golden Ally, you must meet the required criteria and choose at least 2 of the optional contributions

Stay Loosely Informed

Required

Participate and be inside of a group chat called the Golden Council for Golden Ally information and requests! This ensures you stay updated on important announcements and collaboration opportunities.

Atleast 200 members

Required

You require at least 100 real members in your discord server / network.

Public Community Server

Required

Must have an active Discord or platform open to the public - not a closed friend group.

A Pro-Realms Contribution

Required

Must have some form of public contribution to the Pro-Realms community. Including but not limited to: Creating a functional anti-cheat or moderation system, Actively protecting another server from harm or raids, Providing security resources to Realm owners, Hosting or contributing to a database of bad actors, Supporting or leading Realm advocacy efforts within Discord or Minecraft.

Create a Custom Channel or Pinned Post for VSH

Optional

Add a channel or message that links to https://discord.gg/vsh and introduces VSH to your community. This helps your members discover our network while showing your alliance.

Use Golden Era Materials

Optional

Show support with official VSH graphics, tags, or banners in your layout or media. We'll provide you with high-quality assets to represent our partnership.

Co-Host or Join Joint Events

Optional

Help organize or participate in collab events like PvP tournaments, races, or server showcases. These events bring our communities together for memorable experiences.

Announce Key VSH Campaigns

Optional

Repost major Golden Era updates or campaign achievements to your announcements. This helps spread important news while showing your support.

Respond to VSH Requests When Able

Optional

Help when asked, whether it's quick feedback, a boost, repost, or event appearance. We understand you're busy, so this is only when you're available.

Display the Golden Ally Title

Optional

List "Golden Ally of VSH" somewhere visible, in your partner list, description, or a public channel. This public recognition benefits both our communities.

Support VSH Financially

Optional

Optional support via server boosts, small donations, or ad credit, appreciated, never required. This helps us maintain and grow our network.

Publicly Follow VSH Announcements

Optional

Go the extra mile by having a public VSH announcements follow or embed in your server. This keeps your community informed about our joint initiatives.

Why Become a Golden Ally?

Being a Golden Ally means building strength through unity. Here's what you gain by joining our alliance:

Cross-Promotion

Gain exposure through VSH's growing community and network of allies. We'll promote you as we promote ourselves.

Credibility

Be recognized as part of an official VSH campaign, adding prestige to your community's reputation.

Exclusive Access

Get early access to our collab tools, special events, and unique branding opportunities.

Influence

Have a say in the evolution of VSH's Golden Era and help shape the future of our community.

Ready to Join the Golden Alliance?

Let's shape the future of the Minecraft Bedrock community together. Your participation makes our network stronger.
