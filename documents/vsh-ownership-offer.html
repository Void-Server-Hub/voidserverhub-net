Network Regulation

Effective November 19, 2024

Exclusive Server Integration Authority

Only the VSH Owner, XxVoidicxX, has the exclusive authority to bring other servers into the VSH Network. Integrated servers will regulate themselves through their own staff and owner. VSH staff cannot moderate or control subservers directly. Subservers must adhere to VSH regulations to maintain consistency across the network. This ensures autonomy, while preserving the integrity and structure of the Void Network.

This policy creates a balanced ecosystem where each server maintains its identity while benefiting from network resources.
