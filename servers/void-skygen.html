Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Void Skygen

What platforms is Void Skygen available on?

Void Skygen is available on all Minecraft Bedrock Edition platforms, including Windows 10, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, and mobile devices.

Is there a cost to play on Void Skygen?

No, Void Skygen is completely free to play. You only need a legitimate copy of Minecraft Bedrock Edition.

How often is the server updated?

We release major updates every 1-2 months with new content, and smaller patches as needed for bug fixes and balance changes.

Are there any rules I should know about?

Yes, we have simple rules to ensure everyone has a good time: no hacking/cheating, be respectful to others, and no griefing. Full rules are available in our Discord server.

Can I suggest new features for Void Skygen?

Absolutely! We welcome all suggestions in our Discord server's suggestions channel. Many of our current features came from player suggestions.
